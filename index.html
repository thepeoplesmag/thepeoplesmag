Us, You, and Them

The People’s Mag is an electronic magazine for those misplanted souls who are discovering new and strange things in life. We are the magazine for those who jump headfirst into new lands, struggling with the third tone or rolling their tongue. We are here for all poets, fictionals, nonfictionals, and artists who run towards new things because they can. We are here to tune the radio of our diverse world.

At this moment we are accepting submissions for poetry, nonfiction, creative nonfiction, fiction, and digital design. We ask that anyone who wishes to submit should take a gander at our submission guidelines before submitting. We do not wish to waste your or our time on incorrect submissions.
